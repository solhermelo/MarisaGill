Marisa Gill

I was born in Buenos Aires, Argentina, in 1959. Having graduated from The National School of Fine Arts Manuel Belgrano, she improved all the classic graphic techniques with Maestro Alfredo de Vincenzo and Margarita Galetar. She also studied photoengraving, photography, and attended courses and seminars on Art History and Curatorship. Since 1987 she has been participating in national and international art exhibitions and fairs. At the moment, she lives and works in the city of Buenos Aires.
